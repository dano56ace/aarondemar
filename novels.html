About my Graphic Novels

I always loved comic books growing up and i have wanted to create my very own ever since. Being highly influenced by the Japanese culture i began to form worlds and stories that i would sit and create for hours. Like any artist my work must be perfect and being the perfectionist that i am , i am still working out a few issues with my current project.Throughout the years there have been many obstacles but i managed to overcome them with a little patience and effort. I am currently working om my graphic novel and it will be available soon, please wait a little longer.
